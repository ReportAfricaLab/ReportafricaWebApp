# How to Make Money on ReportAfrica
### A Guide for Video Marketing Content

---

## What is ReportAfrica?

ReportAfrica is Africa's citizen-powered live reporting platform. Anyone with a smartphone can report what's happening around them — and GET PAID for it.

---

## 5 Ways to Earn Money on ReportAfrica

---

### 1. GET TIPPED ON YOUR REPORTS

How it works:
- You post a report (text, photo, or video) about something happening in your area
- Other users see your report and can tip you if they find it valuable
- You receive 80% of every tip — directly to your bank account

Example:
You report a major road accident on Third Mainland Bridge. Your report gets 5,000 views. 20 people tip you N500 each.
YOU EARN: N8,000 (80% of N10,000 in tips)

What makes tips come in:
- Breaking news reports (first on the scene)
- Clear photos/videos
- Helpful community information
- Verified, accurate reports

---

### 2. EARN LIVE STREAM GIFTS

How it works:
- Go Live on ReportAfrica to stream what's happening in real-time
- Viewers watching your stream can send you tips/gifts while you're live
- You receive 70% of every live tip

Example:
You go live during an election at your polling unit. 200 people watch. 30 viewers send tips during your stream.
YOU EARN: N14,000 (70% of N20,000 in live tips)

Best times to go live:
- Elections
- Protests
- Traffic situations
- Breaking news
- Community events

---

### 3. LICENSE YOUR MEDIA TO NEWS COMPANIES

How it works:
- You capture exclusive footage of a major event
- Media companies (CNN, Channels TV, BBC Africa) want to use your footage
- They pay a licensing fee through the app
- You receive 50% of the licensing fee

Example:
You capture exclusive video of a building collapse. A TV station pays N200,000 to license it.
YOU EARN: N100,000 (50% of licensing fee)

What media companies look for:
- Exclusive footage (you're the only one who captured it)
- Clear, steady video
- Breaking news moments
- Election irregularities
- Natural disasters

---

### 4. CLIMB THE LEADERBOARD

How it works:
- Every report you submit earns you Trust Points
- Verified reports earn more points
- Reports that get upvoted earn even more
- High Trust Score = higher visibility = more tips

Trust Points breakdown:
- Submit a report: +5 points
- Report gets verified by community: +25 points
- Report gets upvoted: +2 points per upvote
- Referral reward (friend posts first report): +25 points

Benefits of high Trust Score:
- Your reports appear higher in the feed (more views = more tips)
- Unlock media licensing opportunities
- Become a "Trusted Reporter" with verified badge on your profile
- Get invited to cover paid events

---

### 5. REFER FRIENDS AND EARN

How it works:
- Share your referral code with friends
- When they join and post their first report, you earn 25 Trust Points
- Higher trust = more visibility = more earning potential

Your referral code is in the app: Profile > Referral > Share via WhatsApp, SMS, etc.

## Pro Tips for Earning More

1. Be first - First reporter on the scene gets the most tips
2. Go live - Live streams earn more than text reports
3. Be accurate - Verified reports get 5x more visibility
4. Use video - Video reports get 3x more engagement than text
5. Report consistently - Daily reporters build loyal followers who tip regularly
6. Add your bank details - Tips can't reach you without bank info in your profile
7. Share your reports - More views = more tips from new people

## How You Get Paid

- Tips and earnings are paid directly to your Nigerian bank account via KoraPay
- Add your bank details: Profile > Bank Details
- Payments are processed automatically when you receive tips
- Minimum payout: N500
- Supported: All Nigerian banks (GTBank, Access, UBA, First Bank, etc.)
- Other countries: Ghana (GHS), Kenya (KES), South Africa (ZAR), etc.
